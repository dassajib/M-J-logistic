About Us

M & J Logistic Services Ltd. is one of the leading freight forwarding companies in the country. Since its inception in 2014, M & J has never looked back, rather its activities, scope of work and worldwide network has only progressed forward in the past few years. Over time we have moved to diversified business areas like freight forwarding, warehouse maintaining, project handling etc.

Maintaining strong bondage with our partners while giving highest priorities to their businesses is our prime motto. We are committed to help our customers achieving their business goals by providing them the ultimate logistic experience.

M & J always stands by its clients during any logistics challenges and supports its best till the problem gets resolved. We believe our customers are the king and ensure their satisfaction in the right way.




Mission

To become country's most preferred one stop logistics service provider by ensuring professional, reliable and time bound services to satisfy our customers.






Vision

To become one of the renowned global logistics service provider by serving as many customers as possible and diversify business to other sectors of freight forwarding, logistics & supply chain.
