    Incoterms Table (2020)

    Incoterms are an international classification set up to harmonize international trade. They make it possible to codify the duties, rights and responsibilities of importers and exporters in order to:

    •  The loading of the goods

    •  Types of transport

    •  The delivery

    •  Risks (as well as costs)

    •  Insurance policies

    •  Formalities (transport document)
